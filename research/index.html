Time Lens Photon Doppler Velocimetry (TL-PDV)

Photon Doppler velocimetry (PDV) is a widely used diagnostic for measuring velocities arising from shock physics experiments. However, due to limited electrical bandwidth, conventional PDV systems suffer from limited velocity dynamic range when measuring extreme phenomena in shock physics. Time lens PDV (TL-PDV) overcomes these limitations by temporally magnifying the Doppler shifted beat frequencies allowing for recording with lower bandwidth electronics.
